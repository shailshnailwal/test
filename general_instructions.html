General instructions for couples

  • Always visit the centre only after making prior appointment
  • Carry all the previous treatment records
  • Do not hide any relevant medical information
  • Ask your doctor regarding the possible treatment options, its success rate, complications and expenses
  • Be prompt in reporting for follow up visits
  • Understand all prescription instructions before leaving the centre
  • Tell your doctor regarding allergy for any medicines in the past
  • Avoid too many relatives in accompanying you while visiting the doctor
  • Listen the instructions for injections
  • Report to the doctor in time in case of any emergencie
